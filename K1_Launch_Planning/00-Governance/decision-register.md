## D5: Pricing — Detail

**Hypothesis:** K1 can command $449–549 for a 100-unit Founders Edition.
**Prior:** Was $249. Captain is pressure-testing upward.
**Open questions:**
- What do comparable audio-reactive / music visualisation products charge? → **ANSWERED (Cycle 1):** $8–35 (commodity), $135–200 (premium visualiser), $299–750 (pro DJ controllers)
- At $449+, what does the customer expect beyond the product itself (packaging, unboxing, support)? → **OPEN**
- Is there a ceiling where "LED controller" mental model caps willingness-to-pay, regardless of capability? → **PARTIALLY ANSWERED:** Yes, ~$200 ceiling if categorised as "LED controller." Reframing to "music technology" or "design object" required.
- What reference points from adjacent categories (DJ gear, studio monitors, hue lighting) set price anchors? → **ANSWERED (Cycle 1):** SoundSwitch $499 MSRP, Nanoleaf $200, Audiorays $199

**New dependency identified (Cycle 1):** D3 (manifesto enemy) and D1 (colour palette) must resolve first — they determine category positioning, which is the gate for pricing viability.
