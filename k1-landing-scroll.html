What makes K1 different

LIGHT GUIDE PLATE

Not a screen. Not a strip. A 51mm diffused optical surface that produces volumetric, liquid colour. No visible LEDs. No pixels. Just light with depth.

BEAT-TRACKED

Onset detection and PLL beat tracking lock the light to the music’s actual rhythm. Not volume-reactive. Beat-reactive. The difference is everything.

CENTRE-ORIGIN

Every pattern radiates from the centre outward — or collapses inward. No linear scrolling. The light breathes, blooms, and pulses like a living thing.

ZERO LATENCY

256-bin FFT audio pipeline running at 120 FPS on dedicated silicon. The light doesn’t follow the music — it moves with it.
