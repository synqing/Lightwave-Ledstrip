# K1 Launch Research Cycle Log

## Cycle: 2026-03-06 (automated)
- **Decision targeted:** D5 — Pricing ($449–549 range)
- **Research action:** Competitive pricing landscape validation. Mapped every identifiable product in the music-reactive LED space from $130 to $945 to test whether the $449–549 range is defensible.
- **Findings:**
  - [FACT] No consumer product exists at $449–549. Below $200: commodity beat-pulse products (Govee $190, Nanoleaf $200, LIFX $150, Audiorays $199, Divoom $130–160). Above $900: MaestroDMX at $945 (requires separate DMX fixtures).
  - [FACT] SoundSwitch Control One at $299 is controller-only (no LEDs/fixtures).
  - [FACT] MaestroDMX at $945 is the only product with comparable "musical intelligence" claim, but targets DJ/venue market and requires DMX ecosystem.
  - [FACT] Audiorays Pro at $199 with 24-band FFT is the most musically sophisticated product under $200 — and it's 2.2–2.7x cheaper than proposed K1 pricing.
  - [INFERENCE] K1's total-system value (controller + 320 LEDs + LGP + 256-bin FFT + beat/onset/chroma) competes favourably with DMX ecosystem total cost ($1,500–3,000+).
  - [FACT] Consumer resistance to $400+ smart products is documented (NIQ research), but 100-unit Founders Edition bypasses mass-market dynamics.
- **Juxtapositions surfaced:**
  1. K1 intelligence gap: between $200 commodity and $945 professional — void or graveyard?
  2. Audiorays 24-band FFT at $199 vs K1 256-bin FFT at $449–549 — is the sophistication perceptible?
  3. Total-system framing vs wall-panel comparison — which comparison the customer makes determines whether the price is "expensive" or "cheap"
  4. 100-unit scarcity makes this a community question, not a market research question
- **Decision register impact:** No change to D5 status. Evidence is mixed — supports the price if framed against DMX ecosystem; challenges it if framed against Govee/Nanoleaf. The framing IS the decision.
- **Next cycle should:** Research the demonstrability question — find examples of products where audio analysis sophistication was successfully communicated in short-form video. Also investigate: what do the 100 most likely K1 buyers currently own and what did they pay for it?
