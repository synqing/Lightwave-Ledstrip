06

GLASSMORPHISM TREATMENTS

Three glass treatment tiers for layered surfaces. Used for bottom sheets, overlays, and floating controls. All treatments include backdrop-filter blur to let underlying LED colour bleed through, reinforcing the connection between the app UI and the physical light.

LIGHT GLASS
Primary overlays, bottom sheets, floating toolbars. Highest opacity for readability with gentle blur.
background: rgba(255,255,255,0.06) backdrop-filter: blur(24px) border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1)
MEDIUM GLASS
Secondary layers, popover menus, context sheets. More transparency for visual depth.
background: rgba(255,255,255,0.04) backdrop-filter: blur(40px) border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.06)
TINTED GLASS
Brand-tinted overlays, active selection states, emphasis panels. Gold tint connects to brand identity.
background: rgba(255,184,77,0.05) backdrop-filter: blur(32px) border: 1px solid rgba(255,184,77,0.12)
