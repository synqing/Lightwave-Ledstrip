Design system specimen. 13 colour tokens, 3 typefaces, 8 size/weight combinations. Brand yellow is the accent — it marks active states and the BPM hero metric. The hierarchy runs from 44px hero text down to 15px dimmed status, creating a clear reading order even at arm's length.
